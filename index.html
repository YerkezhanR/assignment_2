Central Asia Model United Nations

Model UN Central Asia is an international dialogue platform, held in the mode of discussion of global ideas within the UN, where students & senior school students reproduce the work of the UN bodies.The purpose of each meeting is to discuss the given issues, as well as to prepare and adopt a resolution within the competence of each Committee.
